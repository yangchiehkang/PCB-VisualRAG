11. PCB stack up

4-layer FR4 PCB is sufficient for PTN3356 DP-VGA layout. Suggested layers:
1. Signal layer 1 for DPs, with ground cover the entire buck converter area plus power
islands or thick traces for Vin and Vout for buck converter.
2. Ground layer

3. Power layer with VDD_3V3 islands to cover entire PTN3356, except pin 31, 28, 27
and pin 6.

4. Signal layer 2
